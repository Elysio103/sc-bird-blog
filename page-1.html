About Us

This site offers as a guide
for those in South Carolina
wanting to learn about the birds in the area.
I cover a small sample of what you might expect
whether it's land or sea.

Our Birds

Whether you're a local resident
that has never picked up the hobby,
or a birdwatching enthusiast traveling by,
South Carolina has an amazing variety of bird species.
Whether you're looking for backyard birds,
finding our avians in the forests,
or taking time at the beach to look at shore and sea birds,
there's always an adventure of discovery and wonder to be had.
